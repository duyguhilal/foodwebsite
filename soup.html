    TARHANA SOUP
    Ingredients:
    • 1 cup tarhana
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1 potato, diced
    • 2 tomatoes, grated
    • 1/2 cup red pepper paste
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 6 cups water or vegetable broth
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    Instructions:
    1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent
    2. Add the red pepper paste and continue to sauté for a couple of minutes until it releases its aroma.
    3. Stir in the grated tomatoes and cook until the mixture thickens.
    4. Add the diced carrot and potato to the pot, and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
    5. In a separate bowl, dissolve the tarhana in water or vegetable broth, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
    6. Pour the dissolved tarhana mixture into the pot, stirring constantly to combine.
    7. Add the remaining water or vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are tender.
    8. Season the soup with salt and pepper according to your taste.
    9. Once the soup reaches the desired consistency, remove it from the heat.
    10. Serve the tarhana soup hot, garnished with fresh mint or parsley if desired. Accompany with lemon wedges for a burst of citrus flavor.

    LENTIL SOUP
    Ingredients:
    • 1 cup red lentils
    • 1 onion (finely chopped)
    • 1 carrot (grated)
    • 1 potato (grated)
    • 1 potato (grated)
    • 1 potato (grated)
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon pepper paste (optional)
    • 7 cups water or chicken broth
    • Lemon slices (for serving)
    Instructions:
    1. Rinse and drain the red lentils.
    2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot.
    3. Add the chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
    4. Add the grated carrot and potato, and sauté for a few more minutes.
    5. Stir in the tomato paste and optional pepper paste.
    6. Add the rinsed lentils to the pot. Pour in the water or chicken broth.
    7. Once the mixture boils, reduce the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until the lentils are thoroughly cooked (approximately 20-25 minutes).
    8. Check the consistency of the soup. Add more water if needed.
    9. Season with salt, black pepper, and optional red pepper flakes. Stir well.
    10. Once the soup is simmering, remove it from heat.When serving, you can squeeze a few drops of lemon juice on top.

    YOGURT SOUP
    Ingredıents:
    • 1 cup yogurt
    • 2 tablespoons flour
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 6 cups water or chicken broth
    • Salt
    • Half a lemon (optional)
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    Instructions
    1. In a bowl, whisk together yogurt and flour.
    2. Add the egg yolk and continue to whisk.
    3. In a pot, bring water or chicken broth to a boil. Add the yogurt mixture while stirring continuously.
    4. Let it simmer while stirring until the soup thickens. Add salt to taste.
    5. Optional: Squeeze the juice of half a lemon into the soup and stir. Lemon juice is added for a tangy flavor.
    6. In a separate pan, melt butter. Add red pepper flakes and stir.
    7. Serve the soup in bowls and drizzle the butter and red pepper mixture over the top.

    TOMATO SOUP
    Ingredients:
    • 6 medium-sized tomatoes (grated or chopped)
    • 1 large onion (chopped)
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 2 tablespoons butter or vegetable oil
    • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
    • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1/2 cup milk (optional)
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    Instructions
    1. Melt butter or vegetable oil in a pot. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
    2. Add tomato paste and stir until it releases its aroma.
    3. Add grated or chopped tomatoes. Add sugar, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes soften.
    4. Stir in the flour and cook for a few more minutes over low he
    5. Add chicken or vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 15-20 minutes.
    6. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth.
    7. Optionally, add milk and stir. Cook for a few more minutes.
    8. Garnish with fresh mint or basil before serving.
