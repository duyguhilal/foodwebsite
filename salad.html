    GAVURDAGI SALAD
    Ingredıents:
    • 2 large tomatoes, diced
    • 1 cucumber, diced
    • 1 red onion, finely sliced
    • 1 green bell pepper, diced
    • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
    • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    Instructions:
    1. In a large salad bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, green bell pepper, fresh parsley, and fresh mint.
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, pomegranate molasses (if using), salt, and black pepper until well combined.
    3. Pour the dressing over the prepared vegetables in the salad bowl
    4. Gently toss the salad ingredients until they are well coated with the dressing.
    5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad.
    6. Serve the Gavurdağı Salad immediately, allowing the flavors to meld.

    ROASTED PEPPER SALAD
    Ingredıents:
    • 4 large red or green bell peppers
    • 2 tomatoes, diced
    • 1 cucumber, diced
    • 1 red onion, finely sliced
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    Instructions:
    1. Preheat the grill or oven broiler.
    2. Wash the bell peppers and place them on the grill or under the broiler, turning occasionally until the skin is charred and blistered.
    3. Transfer the grilled peppers to a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let them steam for about 10 minute
    4. Peel off the charred skin, remove seeds, and slice the peppers into thin strips.
    5. In a large salad bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and chopped fresh parsley.
    6. Add the grilled and sliced bell peppers to the bowl.
    7. In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and black pepper.
    8. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients.
    9. Toss the salad gently until all ingredients are well coated with the dressing.
    10. Serve the Közlenmiş Biber Salatası immediately or refrigerate for a short time before serving to enhance the flavors.

    CELERY SALAD
    Ingredients:
    • 4 cups celery, thinly sliced
    • 1 cup green apples, julienned
    • 1 cup walnuts, chopped
    • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped
    • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 1/4 cup Turkish yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
    • 1 tablespoon honey
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    Instructions:
    1. In a large bowl, combine the thinly sliced celery, julienned green apples, chopped walnuts, raisins (if using), and chopped fresh parsley.
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, honey, lemon juice, salt, and black pepper until well combined.
    3. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients.
    4. Toss the salad gently until all ingredients are evenly coated with the dressing.
    5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the celery salad for at least 30 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld.
    6. Serve the Kereviz Salatası chilled as a refreshing side dish.

    KISIR SALAD
    Ingredıents:
    • 2 cups fine bulgur
    • 2.5 cups hot water
    • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
    • 1/4 cup pomegranate molasses
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    • 1/4 cup tomato paste
    • 1 bunch of green onions
    • 1/2 bunch of fresh parsley
    • 1/2 bunch of fresh mint
    • Juice of 1 lemon
    • 2 medium tomatoes
    • 1/2 cucumber
    Instructions:
    1. Place the bulgur in a large bowl. Add hot water and stir. Cover with plastic wrap and let it sit for 15-20 minutes for the bulgur to swell.
    2. Add extra virgin olive oil, pomegranate molasses, salt, red pepper flakes, and tomato paste to the swollen bulgur. Mix well.
    3. For the filling, finely chop green onions, parsley, mint, lemon juice, tomatoes, and cucumber.
    4. Add the chopped ingredients to the bulgur mixture and mix thoroughly.
    5. Transfer the kısır salad to a serving plate. You can also serve it on lettuce leaves if desired.
    6. Serve chilled and enjoy!
